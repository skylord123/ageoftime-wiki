## Towns and structures

### Port Town

![Port Town](assets/areas/5PortTown.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

The starting town. Has a Sword Giver, Shop, Bank, and Blacksmith.
The [Driftwood Inn](#driftwood-inn) and a small nearby house also provide beds.

### Tavern

![Tavern](assets/areas/5Tavern.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

Located near Fort Bad. Sells healing potions. Recycled visually as the
Starboard Shop.

| Item | Price | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| [Blue Vial](items.md#general-items) | 55 gold | Heals a small amount of HP. |
| [Blue Potion](items.md#general-items) | 160 gold | Heals a large amount of HP. Also sold in [Starboard Shop](#starboard-shop). |

### Arena

![Arena](assets/areas/5Arena.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

Near Port Town. A proper PvP arena — dying here does not cause you to lose
Gold. It also acts as a persistent respawn point once visited.

### Starboard Town

![Starboard Town](assets/areas/5StarboardTown.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

A near-identical copy of Port Town with minor aesthetic differences. Sits above a lake.

### Volcano

![Volcano](assets/areas/5Volcano.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

A long narrow tube. You will die instantly to fall damage unless you have a
Horse or a Hook. Contains Orange, Black, and Magenta Dye. Home to Fire Orcs.

### Beach House

![Beach House](assets/areas/5BeachHouse.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

A copy of a house in Port Town. Surrounded by Blue Slimes.

### Fort Bad

![Fort Bad](assets/areas/5FortBad.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

A fort dedicated to Badspot. Rocket Orcs spawn outside.

### Cave

![Cave](assets/areas/5Cave.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

A short pitch-dark cave that ends in a pit with a massive monster spawn rate.

## Challenges and levels

### Treehouse Challenge

A challenge where you must scale up the base of a tree. Used to give you a
Magic Crossbow for sale; **currently broken**.

### Log Challenge

![Log Challenge](assets/areas/5LogChallenge.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

A tall stack of logs near Red Crater. Scale it to obtain the **Golden Hook**.
Also acts as a persistent respawn point once visited.

### Level 1

![Level 1](assets/areas/5Level1.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

A level hidden in the Woods. Awards a **Hook** at the end. If you die inside,
you can respawn in or near it, but it does not stay set once you leave.

### Level 2

A level near Fort Bad. Used to give a large sum of Gold; **currently broken**.

## Major areas

| Area | Description |
|---|---|
| **Woods** | A large wooded area near Port Town. Contains Green Dyes and Level 1. Dying inside can respawn you there or nearby, but it is not a persistent respawn point once you leave. |
| **Swamp** | A large low-water-level area. Contains Cyan Dyes and Zombies. Also contains a large white orb-like object with very high HP. It can be destroyed, but it respawns in the same spot after a short time and is not known to drop gold or serve a gameplay purpose. Players sometimes hide inside it by breaking it and waiting for it to respawn over them. |
| **Auric Fields** | A field of yellow grass. Contains Yellow Dyes. |
| **Red Crater** | A crater full of red grass. Contains Red Dyes. Its gold drop rate appears noticeably worse than most other grinding areas. |
| **Blue Hill** | An underwater crater full of Sea Monsters. Contains Blue Dyes. While you are in water, Sea Monster spawns override other local spawn tables. |
| **Distant Forest** | A very far-away dark forest. Nothing special. |

## Other landmarks

### Woods Tunnels

A dark, narrow series of tunnels inside the Woods. Used to contain a $200 bill and some Gold.

### Police Station

![Police Station](assets/areas/5Police.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

A brick building near Port Town. After being online for an hour without
committing any crimes you can become a police officer here.

### Driftwood Inn

An inn in Port Town and one of the few places where you can be naked without
getting demerits. Contains **three beds**.

### Port Town house near spawn

A small house near the Port Town spawn area. Contains **one bed upstairs**.

### Port Town Shop

also hosts the [player-to-player marketplace](#player-marketplace).
Signs on the building read **Harbor Trading Company**, and the door has a
large **SHOP** sign with an arrow pointing at the entrance.

### Starboard marketplace building

The area where you **list items for sale** in Starboard Town is in a separate
building behind the Starboard Shop. The buying area and selling area are not
the same building.

### Player marketplace

Both the [Port Town Shop](#port-town-shop) and the Starboard Town marketplace
area behind the [Starboard Shop](#starboard-shop) let players list their own
items for sale to other players.

- **Listing fee:** depends on the exact sale spot used.
- **Port Town side sale spots:** 5 gold.
- **Port Town center sale spots:** 10 gold.
- **Port Town single raised middle spot (on the box):** 20 gold.
- **Starboard Town sale spots:** 5 gold.
- **If your item sells:** the gold is delivered to you in the mail.
- **If your item doesn't sell** for an extended period: the unsold item is
  returned to you in the mail.

## Building mechanics

### Doors

Doors appear on various buildings throughout the game. They can be destroyed,
but have high HP and respawn a few seconds later.
