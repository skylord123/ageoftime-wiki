# Volcano

![Volcano](../../assets/areas/5Volcano.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

A long narrow tube containing Orange, Black, and Magenta dye crates. It is
also home to Fire Orcs.

## Notes

- You will die instantly to fall damage unless you have a Horse or a Hook.
- Crates here can drop Gold, the local dye colors, or occasional Dynamite.
