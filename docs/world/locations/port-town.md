# Port Town

![Port Town](../../assets/areas/5PortTown.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

The starting town and main hub of *Age of Time*. Port Town contains the
[Sword Giver](../../npcs/sword-giver.md), [Banker](../../npcs/banker.md),
[Blacksmith](../../npcs/blacksmith.md), [Marshal](../../npcs/police.md), the
main shop, and one half of the player marketplace.

## Overview

- New characters spawn here.
- Visiting Port Town sets it as a persistent respawn point.
- It is the main safe hub for banking, shopping, crafting, and joining the
  police.

## Police Station

![Police Station](../../assets/areas/5Police.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

A brick building near spawn. After being online for an hour without committing
any crimes, you can enlist with the [Marshal](../../npcs/police.md) here.

For laws, demerits, arrests, jail, and fines, see
[Police Mechanics](../../police-mechanics.md).

## Driftwood Inn

An inn in Port Town and one of the few places where you can be naked without
getting demerits. Contains **three beds**.

## House near spawn

A small house near the Port Town spawn area. Contains **one bed upstairs**.

## Shop

![Port Town Shop](../../assets/areas/5PortTownShop.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

The main shop in Port Town. Sells equipment, consumables, and clothing, and
also hosts the [player marketplace](#player-marketplace). Signs on the
building read **Harbor Trading Company**, and the door has a large **SHOP**
sign with an arrow pointing at the entrance.

## Player marketplace

Port Town and Starboard Town both support player listings. Port Town contains
the buying side for its local sale spots, while Starboard Town has a separate
building for listing items.

- **Listing fee:** depends on the exact sale spot used.
- **Port Town side sale spots:** 5 gold.
- **Port Town center sale spots:** 10 gold.
- **Port Town single raised middle spot (on the box):** 20 gold.
- **If your item sells:** the gold is delivered by mail through the
  [Post Office Clerk](../../npcs/post-office.md).
- **If your item does not sell** for an extended period: the unsold item is
  returned by mail.
