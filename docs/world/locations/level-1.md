# Level 1

![Level 1](../../assets/areas/5Level1.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

A level hidden in the [Woods](woods.md).

## Reward

Completing Level 1 awards the [Hook](../../movement-items.md).

## Respawn behavior

- If you die inside Level 1, you can respawn in or very near it.
- Once you leave, Level 1 does **not** remain as a persistent respawn point.
