# Blue Hill

![Blue Hill](../../assets/captures/blue-hill/view-underwater.png){ width=400 loading=lazy }

An underwater crater full of Sea Monsters and Blue Dye crates.

## Notes

- While you are in water, Sea Monster spawns override other local spawn
  tables.
- Crates here can drop Gold, Blue Dye, or occasional Dynamite.
