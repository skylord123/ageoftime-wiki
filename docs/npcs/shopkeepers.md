# Shopkeepers

Three shopkeepers run the static stores in *Age of Time*. Their full
inventories and prices are documented on the [Areas](../areas.md) page.

## Port Town Shopkeeper

Runs the [Port Town Shop](../areas.md#port-town-shop) — the main shop in the
game. Sells equipment, consumables, and clothing, and hosts the
[player marketplace](../areas.md#player-marketplace).

## Starboard Shopkeeper

Runs the [Starboard Shop](../areas.md#starboard-shop) in Starboard Town. The
only place in the game to buy a Thong and Expensive Parchment. Also hosts a
[player marketplace](../areas.md#player-marketplace).

## Tavern Keeper

Runs the [Tavern](../areas.md#tavern) near Fort Bad. Sells healing potions —
notably, the **only** source of [Blue Potions](../items.md#general-items).
The Tavern's interior is visually identical to the Starboard Shop's.
