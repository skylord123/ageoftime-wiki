# Friendly NPCs

Non-hostile characters that the player can interact with around the world of
*Age of Time*. Talk to an NPC by looking at them and pressing the action key
(default <kbd>E</kbd>).

For hostile creatures, see [Enemies](../enemies.md).

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

- :material-sword: **[Sword Giver](sword-giver.md)**

    Hands out a free starter sword on the Port Town docks.

- :material-anvil: **[Blacksmith](blacksmith.md)**

    Crafts weapons, shields, clothing, cloth, and refined metals.

- :material-store: **[Shopkeepers](shopkeepers.md)**

    The Port Town Shop, Starboard Shop, and Tavern keepers.

- :material-bank: **[Banker](banker.md)**

    Deposit and withdraw gold to keep it safe from PvP losses.

- :material-email: **[Post Office Clerk](post-office.md)**

    Handles in-game mail, including marketplace deliveries.

- :material-police-badge: **[Police](police.md)**

    Enlist after an hour of crime-free playtime to receive a Police Baton.

</div>
