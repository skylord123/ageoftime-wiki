# Cloth

Cloth is a crafted material used for clothing recipes at the
[Blacksmith](../npcs/blacksmith.md#clothing).

- All cloth recipes cost **0 gold**.
- Each recipe produces **ClothItem ×5**.
- Cloth itself is **not** directly dyed afterward. Dyes matter either when
  making the cloth pattern or when later coloring finished clothing.

For dyes and color tools, see [Dyes](dyes.md), [Dye Calculator](../dye-calculator.md),
and [Dye Recipe Finder](../dye-recipe-finder.md).

## Recipes

Each recipe combines raw [Fiber](index.md#raw-materials) with one or more dyes
to produce a distinct cloth pattern.
